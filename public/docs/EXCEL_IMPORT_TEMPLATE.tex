\begin{successbox}
    \textbf{\color{successGreen}\Large \faFileExcel \quad EXECUTIVE SUMMARY}
    \vspace{0.3cm}
    
    This document defines the standardized Excel template for importing employee and organizational data from VGG subsidiary companies that do not utilize SeamlessHR or KlevaHR platforms. The template ensures data consistency, enables seamless integration with the People Information System database, and supports group-wide workforce analytics and reporting capabilities.
    
    \vspace{0.2cm}
    
    \textbf{Collaboration Framework:} ProDG provides technical implementation and database architecture, while VGG subsidiaries collect and submit data using this standardized format. This partnership ensures data quality, security, and analytical readiness across all portfolio companies.
\end{successbox}

% ===== Section 1: Workbook Structure =====
\section{\faFolderOpen \quad Workbook Structure Overview}

The Excel workbook must contain \textbf{six (6) standardized sheets}. Each sheet serves a specific purpose in the data collection process:

\vspace{0.3cm}

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{@{}clll@{}}
    \toprule
    \textbf{Sheet \#} & \textbf{Sheet Name} & \textbf{Purpose} & \textbf{Required} \\
    \midrule
    1 & \texttt{Company\_Info} & Company/subsidiary metadata & Yes \\
    2 & \texttt{Employees} & Core employee records & Yes \\
    3 & \texttt{Departments} & Organizational structure & Yes \\
    4 & \texttt{Job\_Roles} & Position definitions & Recommended \\
    5 & \texttt{Leave\_Records} & Leave/time-off history & Optional \\
    6 & \texttt{Attendance} & Attendance tracking & Optional \\
    \bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\begin{importantbox}
    \textbf{\color{vggRed}\faExclamationTriangle \quad Important Notes:}
    \bulletlist{
        \bulletitem{Sheet names must match \textbf{exactly} as specified (case-sensitive)}
        \bulletitem{Do not add extra sheets or rename existing sheets}
        \bulletitem{All dates must use \texttt{YYYY-MM-DD} format}
        \bulletitem{Boolean fields must use \texttt{TRUE} or \texttt{FALSE} only}
        \bulletitem{No empty rows between data rows}
    }
\end{importantbox}

\section{\faClipboardCheck \quad Pre-Import Checklist}

\begin{importantbox}
Before submitting your Excel file, verify the following:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=20pt]
    \item[$\square$] All required fields are populated for every employee
    \item[$\square$] All dates are in \texttt{YYYY-MM-DD} format
    \item[$\square$] Employee codes are unique (no duplicates)
    \item[$\square$] Department names match exactly across all sheets
    \item[$\square$] Email addresses are valid and unique
    \item[$\square$] Status values match the allowed options exactly
    \item[$\square$] Numbers are plain numbers (no currency symbols or commas)
    \item[$\square$] Boolean fields use only \texttt{TRUE} or \texttt{FALSE}
    \item[$\square$] No empty rows between data rows
    \item[$\square$] Sheet names match exactly as specified
    \item[$\square$] File saved as \texttt{.xlsx} format
\end{enumerate}
\end{importantbox}
